Z.E.N Project - Zambian Education Network

Tomorrow's Learning, Today

Education Software Solutions

Z.E.N Project (Zambian Education Network) is a specialized division of MiThn founded by Febian Moono, focused on revolutionizing education in Zambia through intelligent, accessible, and integrated digital tools.

Our vision is to empower schools and educational institutions across Zambia with technology that enhances learning experiences, simplifies administration, and bridges educational gaps in underserved communities.
